Welcome to Fundoralit.

Fundoralit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how Fundoralit collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the Fundoralit mobile application and related services.

By using Fundoralit, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Fundoralit follows data minimization principles and collects only the information necessary to provide application functionality and related services.

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Authentication information provided through Firebase Authentication
  • Profile information you choose to provide

1.2 Financial and Usage Information

To provide personal finance management features, Fundoralit may collect:

  • Expense records
  • Income records
  • Budget and saving goals
  • Bill reminders and payment tracking
  • Wallet and bucket allocations
  • Group event and group saving information
  • Financial analysis and projections generated from your data

1.3 Device and Technical Information

We may automatically collect limited technical information including:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs
  • Diagnostic information
  • Notification permission status

Some information may also be securely stored locally on your device using local databases or device storage to support offline functionality and improve app performance.

If biometric login features are enabled, authentication-related credentials may be securely stored on the device to support faster sign-in and improve account security.

Receipt scanning features may use optical character recognition (OCR) technology to extract expense-related information from images captured by users.

Fundoralit does not use receipt images or financial information for advertising purposes.

1.4 Notification Access (Android Only)

If you enable Smart Capture features on Android devices, Fundoralit may process supported transaction-related notification content locally on your device to help detect financial transactions and simplify expense tracking.

Fundoralit does not intentionally access unrelated personal messages or store raw notification content on remote servers.

Users may disable notification access permissions at any time through Android device settings.

1.5 Cloud Backup Information

If you enable cloud backup or synchronization features, selected financial and application data may be securely transmitted and stored on cloud infrastructure to support:

  • Backup and restore
  • Multi-device synchronization
  • Data recovery

Cloud backup data is protected using industry-standard security measures and encrypted network communication.

2. How We Use Your Information

Fundoralit uses collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Sync and restore user data
  • Generate financial insights and analytics
  • Improve budgeting and saving experiences
  • Enable group financial collaboration features
  • Send reminders and notifications
  • Improve app stability, performance, and security
  • Detect abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access
  • Manage subscription, premium access, and entitlement-related features

Fundoralit does not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes.

We may use aggregated or anonymized information for analytics and product improvement purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure authentication systems
  • Encrypted network communication (HTTPS)
  • Access control and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure cloud infrastructure providers
  • Local device storage protections where applicable

However, no internet-based service or electronic storage system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Fundoralit may use trusted third-party services to support app functionality.

These services may process limited data as required for their services.

Examples include:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Supabase
  • Google Play Services
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Analytics and crash reporting providers

Firebase Authentication is used for secure account sign-in and authentication management.

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

Fundoralit does not sell personal information.

We may share limited information only in the following situations:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the security and integrity of the application
  • With trusted service providers that help operate the platform
  • With your consent

6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide services
  • Maintain backups and synchronization
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Some information may remain in backups or archived systems for a limited period before permanent deletion.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have rights to:

  • Access your information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw permissions
  • Disable notifications
  • Stop using cloud synchronization features

Users may revoke application permissions through device settings at any time.

8. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion directly within the Fundoralit application.

When an account deletion request is submitted:

  • Authentication credentials may be removed
  • Associated financial records may be deleted
  • Cloud backup data may be removed
  • Group-related data may be anonymized or preserved where necessary to protect other users' experience

Some limited records may be temporarily retained for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or system integrity purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

Fundoralit is not intended for children under the age required by local laws to consent to digital services.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. International Data Processing

Your information may be processed or stored in countries outside your own jurisdiction depending on the cloud infrastructure providers used by Fundoralit.

By using Fundoralit, you consent to such transfers where permitted by law.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Updated versions will be posted within the application or associated official pages with a revised "Last Updated" date.

For significant changes, we may notify users through the application or other appropriate communication methods.

Continued use of Fundoralit after updates means you accept the revised Privacy Policy.
