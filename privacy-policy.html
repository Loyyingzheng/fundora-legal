Welcome to Fundoralit.

Fundoralit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how Fundoralit collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your information when you use the Fundoralit mobile application and related services.

Fundoralit is a personal finance management app. Because financial information can be sensitive, we aim to collect only the information needed to provide app features, protect your account, maintain subscriptions, support backup and restore, and improve service reliability.

By using Fundoralit, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Fundoralit follows data minimization principles and collects only the information necessary to provide application functionality, security, backup, synchronization, subscription, and related services.

1.1 Account Information

When you create or sign in to an account, we may collect or process:

  • Email address
  • Username, display name, or profile name you choose to provide
  • Authentication identifiers provided through Firebase Authentication
  • User IDs and account identifiers used to operate the service
  • Profile information, preferences, or other account information you choose to provide
  • Subscription and entitlement status, where applicable

1.2 Financial and App Content

To provide personal finance management features, Fundoralit may collect or store information you enter or generate in the app, including:

  • Expense records
  • Income records
  • Transfer records
  • Wallet and bucket allocations
  • Budget and saving goals
  • Bill reminders and payment tracking
  • Destination or saving target information
  • Group event, shared expense, participant, settlement, and group saving information
  • Financial analysis, summaries, and projections generated from your data
  • App settings and preferences needed to operate the service

1.3 Subscription and Purchase-Related Information

If you use Pro, trials, feedback reward trials, or Google Play subscription features, Fundoralit may process purchase-related information needed to verify entitlement and prevent abuse, including:

  • Google Play product ID
  • Purchase token or token hash
  • Order ID, where provided by Google Play
  • Subscription tier, billing cycle, status, expiry, transfer, restore, or cancellation state
  • Feedback reward trial eligibility and redemption status

Fundoralit does not collect your credit card number, bank card details, or full payment method details. Payments and billing are handled by Google Play where applicable.

1.4 Device and Technical Information

We may automatically collect limited technical information to operate, secure, and improve the app, including:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version and build number
  • Device or installation identifiers used for app functionality, policy, diagnostics, security, and abuse prevention
  • Crash logs or diagnostic information, if enabled
  • Notification permission status
  • Feature usage and quota status, where needed to provide limits or premium access

Some information may also be securely stored locally on your device using local databases or device storage to support offline functionality and improve app performance.

If biometric login features are enabled, authentication-related credentials may be securely stored on your device to support faster sign-in and improve account security.

1.5 Receipt Scanning, Photos, and Local OCR

Receipt scanning features may use optical character recognition (OCR) technology to extract expense-related information from images captured or selected by you.

Fundoralit is designed to perform receipt scanning locally on your device where supported. Extracted receipt information may be used to help create or suggest financial entries for your review.

If you use group receipt upload or other upload features, selected receipt images or files may be transmitted to cloud infrastructure to provide the feature you requested.

Fundoralit does not use receipt images or extracted financial information for advertising purposes.

Receipt images and extracted text are not intentionally sold, rented, or used for third-party marketing.

1.6 Files, Documents, Backup, Import, and Export

Fundoralit may process files or documents you choose to upload, import, export, or back up, including backup snapshots and related app data files. These files are used to provide app functionality, backup, restore, synchronization, and data recovery.

1.7 Smart Capture and Notification Access (Android Only)

If you enable Smart Capture on Android devices, Fundoralit may process supported transaction-related notification content locally on your device to help detect financial transactions and simplify expense tracking.

Smart Capture only runs after you grant Android notification access permission. It is intended to detect transaction-related notifications, such as bank, e-wallet, payment, telco, or merchant transaction notifications.

To identify supported transaction providers and allow you to ignore selected apps, Fundoralit may process app package names, app labels, installed app/provider information, and notification source information related to Smart Capture.

Fundoralit does not intentionally access unrelated personal messages and does not intentionally store raw unrelated notification content on remote servers.

Smart Capture may create local review items or suggestions so you can confirm, edit, or reject detected entries before saving them.

You may disable notification access at any time through Android device settings.

1.8 Cloud Backup and Synchronization Information

If you enable cloud backup or synchronization features, selected financial and application data may be securely transmitted and stored on cloud infrastructure to support:

  • Backup and restore
  • Multi-device synchronization
  • Data recovery
  • Service continuity if your device is lost, changed, or reset

New cloud backup snapshots are encrypted before storage using server-side envelope encryption and are not stored as readable plaintext. Backup data is protected in transit using HTTPS.

Cloud Backup is designed to protect your financial records while still allowing account-based restore after you sign in with your Fundoralit account.

2. How We Use Your Information

Fundoralit uses collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Authenticate users and protect accounts
  • Sync, back up, and restore user data
  • Create, display, and manage financial records
  • Generate financial insights, summaries, and analytics
  • Improve budgeting, saving, Smart Capture, OCR, and bill tracking experiences
  • Enable group financial collaboration features
  • Send reminders, alerts, and app notifications
  • Verify Google Play subscription purchases, Pro entitlement, free trials, and reward trials
  • Manage subscription, premium access, entitlement, quota, and restore-related features
  • Improve app stability, performance, and security
  • Detect abuse, fraud, unauthorized access, or technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce applicable terms

Fundoralit does not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes.

We may use aggregated or anonymized information for analytics, reliability, security, and product improvement purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure authentication systems
  • Encrypted network communication using HTTPS
  • Access control and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure cloud infrastructure providers
  • Encrypted storage for new Cloud Backup snapshots using server-side envelope encryption
  • Local device storage protections where applicable
  • Operational safeguards such as audit logging for sensitive administrative actions
  • Measures to reduce unnecessary storage of raw sensitive content

New cloud backup snapshots are encrypted before storage and are not stored as readable plaintext. This helps reduce the risk of exposure if backup storage is accessed without authorization.

However, no internet-based service, mobile application, or electronic storage system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Fundoralit may use trusted third-party services to support app functionality, security, authentication, payments, cloud infrastructure, diagnostics, and app distribution.

These services may process limited data only as required to provide their services.

Examples may include, depending on enabled features:

  • Firebase Authentication for secure account sign-in and authentication management
  • Supabase or managed database infrastructure for secure data storage
  • Google Play Services for subscriptions, purchases, billing, app services, and distribution
  • Cloud hosting providers for backend APIs and infrastructure
  • Crash, diagnostic, or analytics services if enabled to improve stability and reliability

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies and security practices.

Fundoralit does not use receipt images, Smart Capture data, notification content, or financial records for third-party advertising.

5. Data Sharing

Fundoralit does not sell personal information.

We may share limited information only in the following situations:

  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect the security, rights, and integrity of the application, users, or platform
  • With trusted service providers that help operate the platform
  • With your consent or at your direction
  • As necessary to provide group collaboration features you choose to use

For group events and shared financial features, information you enter for a group may be visible to other group members as necessary to provide the collaboration feature. For example, shared expenses, participants, amounts, receipt attachments, and settlement information may be visible to relevant group members.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide services
  • Maintain backups and synchronization
  • Maintain account, subscription, entitlement, and security records
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access

Some information may remain in backups, logs, or archived systems for a limited period before permanent deletion, subject to technical, security, legal, billing, fraud prevention, or operational requirements.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have rights to:

  • Access your information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw permissions
  • Disable notifications
  • Disable Android notification access for Smart Capture
  • Stop using cloud backup or synchronization features
  • Manage subscription features through Google Play, where applicable

Users may revoke application permissions through device settings at any time. Some app features may not work properly if required permissions are disabled.

8. Account Deletion

Users can request account and data deletion from within the Fundoralit application.

When an account deletion request is submitted:

  • Authentication credentials may be removed or disabled
  • Associated personal financial records may be deleted
  • Cloud backup data may be removed
  • Subscription and entitlement records may be retained where required for billing, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, refund handling, or legal compliance
  • Group-related data may be anonymized, deleted, or preserved where necessary to protect other users' experience and preserve shared group records

Some limited records may be temporarily retained for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, backup integrity, billing integrity, or system integrity purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

Fundoralit is not intended for children under the age required by local laws to consent to digital services.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child without appropriate consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete such information.
