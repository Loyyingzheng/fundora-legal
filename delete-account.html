Fundora Account Deletion

Learn how to delete your Fundora account and request data removal.

Delete your account in the app

The fastest way to delete your Fundora account is directly in the app:

  1. Open the Fundora app
  2. Go to Profile → Security → Delete Account
  3. Follow the prompts to confirm deletion

This method allows for immediate verification and faster processing.

Request deletion without app access

If you can no longer access the Fundora app, you can request account deletion by emailing us from your registered email address.

We may need to verify your account ownership before processing the request.

Request Account Deletion

What will be deleted

When your account is deleted, the following personal data will be removed:

  • Account credentials and authentication information
  • Personal profile information
  • Financial records (expenses, income, budgets)
  • Personal saving goals and progress
  • Wallet and bucket allocations
  • Bill reminders and payment tracking
  • Cloud backup data
  • Biometric login records
  • Local app session data

Shared group records

Some shared Group Event or Group Goal records may remain because they are part of other members' shared financial history. Where retained, your identity will be anonymized as "Deleted user", and personal identifiers such as email and login credentials will be removed.

Data we may retain

For legal, security, or operational reasons, some limited data may be retained temporarily:

  • Anonymized usage statistics
  • Records needed for dispute resolution or legal compliance
  • Data required for fraud prevention

Retained data is not linked to your personal identity and will be deleted after the required retention period.

Processing time

Account deletions requested through the app are processed immediately. Email requests may take up to 30 days to process, depending on verification requirements.
